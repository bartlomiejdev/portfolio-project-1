Welcome to Auto Detailing Portlaoise!

We are one of the longest established car cleaning and car valets in Portlaoise. We have been cleaning cars for 25 years, since 1995, and our car detailers are some of the best in Ireland. We offer high-end car detailing and car cleaning services for all makes and models. In fact, we have cleaned anything from a small hatchback to a Ferrari 360. Drawing on 25 years of experience, and using only the best products (Swissvax, C-Quartz, Polish Angel, etc.), enables us to give results that impress. We mainly operate in Portlaoise, Leinster, and offer nationwide detailing services for high-end jobs. Contact us to find out more about our services or to book your car in for a professional clean.
